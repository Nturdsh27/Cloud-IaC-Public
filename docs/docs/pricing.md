## FAQ

**Can I use the free tier in production?**
Yes. The Community tier is production-ready — it's just scoped to simpler architectures (single project, basic scanning).

**What happens if I stop paying?**
You keep everything you've deployed. The Terraform modules don't phone home. You lose access to updates and support.

**Can I upgrade mid-month?**
Yes. You're prorated for the remainder of the billing cycle.

**Do you offer discounts for annual billing?**
Yes — 2 months free on annual plans (17% discount).
